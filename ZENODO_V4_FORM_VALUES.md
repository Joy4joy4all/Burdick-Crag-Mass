BETELGEUSE: Red supergiant m=3 from giant convective cells confirmed by Hamiltonian minimum. Interpreted as transition from wave-dominated to convection-dominated substrate regime. Great Dimming 2019-2020 reframed as substrate mode event — directed mass ejection at one of three azimuthal nodes.
