### Safety Redefinition (Gemini — confirmed Burdick)

Safe arrival corridor is NOT defined by absolute σ level.
Safe = min(|∇σ|) — minimum gradient magnitude.

The gradient is what couples to the craft. High σ with
smooth gradient = laminar entry. Low σ with sharp
gradient = frequency generator. The anti-throat is safest
because the substrate there has no drain pulling it toward
L1, producing the smoothest gradient profile.
